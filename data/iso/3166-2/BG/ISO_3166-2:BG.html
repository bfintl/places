ISO 3166-2:BG

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Jump to: navigation, search

ISO 3166-2:BG is an ISO standard which defines geocodes: it is the subset of ISO 3166-2 which applies to Bulgaria.

The provinces (oblasti, singular - oblast) are numbered according to their position in the Bulgarian alphabet.
