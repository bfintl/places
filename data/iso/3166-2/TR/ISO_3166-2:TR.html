ISO 3166-2:TR

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Jump to: navigation, search

ISO 3166-2:TR is an International Organization for Standardization standard which defines geocodes: it is the subset of ISO 3166-2 which applies to Turkey.

[edit] Newsletters

  • ISO 3166-2:2000-06-21
  • ISO 3166-2:2002-05-21

[edit] Codes

The codes are given per province and are of the format TR-XX where XX is the license plate code of the province.
