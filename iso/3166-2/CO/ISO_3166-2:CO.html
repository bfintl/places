ISO 3166-2:CO

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Jump to: navigation, search

ISO 3166-2:CO is an ISO standard which defines geocodes: it is the subset of ISO 3166-2 which applies to Colombia. It covers the 32 departments (three-letter) and the capital territory (two-letter).
