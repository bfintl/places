ISO 3166-2:TZ

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Jump to: navigation, search

ISO 3166-2:TZ is an ISO standard which defines geocodes: it is the subset of ISO 3166-2 which applies to the Tanzania. It covers the 26 regions mkoa (sw)

  • List source: IGN 1990; update BET 2003 (Information from French Embassy to Dar es Salaam, 2003-04)
  • Code source: Tanzania Bureau of Standards (TBS), 1988-05-16; ISO 3166/MA (*)
