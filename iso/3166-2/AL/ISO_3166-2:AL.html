ISO 3166-2:AL

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Jump to: navigation, search

ISO 3166-2:AL is an ISO standard which defines geocodes: it is the subset of ISO 3166-2 which applies to Albania.

It covers the 36 rrethe (districts). The districts are grouped together in qark (counties), which are not encoded.
