ISO 3166-2:HR

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Jump to: navigation, search

ISO 3166-2:HR is an ISO standard which defines geocodes: it is the subset of ISO 3166-2 which applies to Croatia. It covers 20 counties and 1 city.
