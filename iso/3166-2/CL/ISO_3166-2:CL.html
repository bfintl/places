ISO 3166-2:CL

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Jump to: navigation, search

ISO 3166-2:CL is an ISO standard which defines geocodes: it is the subset of ISO 3166-2 which applies to Chile.

[edit] Codes

CL-AI Aisén del General Carlos Ibañez (XI)
CL-AN Antofagasta (II)
CL-AR Araucanía (IX)
CL-AT Atacama (III)
CL-BI Bío-Bío (VIII)
CL-CO Coquimbo (IV)
CL-LI O'Higgins (VI)
CL-LL Los Lagos (X)
CL-MA Magallanes y la Antártica Chilena (XII)
CL-ML Maule (VII)
CL-RM Santiago Metropolitan Region (RM)
CL-TA Tarapacá (I)
CL-VS Valparaíso (V)
Note 
Each region is designated by a name and a Roman numeral. Numbers are assigned from north to south. In general the Roman numeral is used, rather than the name. The only exception is the region where Santiago is situated, which is designated RM.
