ISO 3166-2:DO

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Jump to: navigation, search

ISO 3166-2:DO is an ISO standard which defines geocodes: it is the subset of ISO 3166-2 which applies to the Dominican Republic.

There are no ISO-codes for:

  • Santo Domingo (created 2001 from Distrito Nacional)
  • San José de Ocoa (created 2002 from Peravia)
