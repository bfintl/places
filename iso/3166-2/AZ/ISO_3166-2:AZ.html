ISO 3166-2:AZ

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Jump to: navigation, search

ISO 3166-2:AZ is an ISO standard which defines geocodes: it is the subset of ISO 3166-2 which applies to Azerbaijan. It describes 1 autonomous republic, 11 cities, and 65 rayons.
